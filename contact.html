Frequently Asked Questions

What are your minimum order quantities?

Our minimum order quantities vary by product type and complexity. For detailed garments, we typically work with orders of 100+ pieces, while custom embroidered items may have higher minimums. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What are typical production lead times?

Standard production times range from 4-8 weeks depending on complexity and quantity. Rush orders may be accommodated with additional planning. We'll provide accurate timelines based on your specific requirements.

Do you handle international shipping?

Yes, we have extensive experience in international export and can handle all shipping logistics, documentation, and customs requirements for deliveries worldwide.

Can you work with our designs?

Absolutely! We work with client-provided designs, tech packs, and specifications. We also offer design development services if you need support bringing your concepts to life.

What quality certifications do you have?

We maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification and comply with international quality standards including OEKO-TEX, GOTS, and WRAP certifications for responsible manufacturing.

Do you offer samples?

Yes, we can provide samples for evaluation. Sample costs and timelines depend on complexity. We often offer sample cost credits toward production orders.
